DTOC: Donate To Our Cause

Mission Statement:

We are two high school students, Priyanshu Sharma and Advait Sharma, dedicated to making a positive impact on our community. Our goal is to simplify the donation process, allowing people to contribute from the comfort of their homes. By making it easier to donate items, we hope to encourage more people to help those in need and spread kindness throughout our community. Thank you for joining us in this mission.

At DTOC, we simplify donating by letting you share your name, address, and items through our website. Update weekly, and our team will pick up your donations for local shelters, food banks, and senior homes. Join us in spreading kindness—your info stays private. Thank you!
